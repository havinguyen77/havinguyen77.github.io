About Me

I started modeling and learning about photography as an outlet to express my creativity. I have opened the runway show for Dynasty George and walked for Identity, Designed and The Pluid Project. My photographs have been featured in the Power of Art Exhibit, the Quench Magazine, and given the highest rating at the Texas Visual Art Scholastic Competition.

My passion also lies in technology, entrepreneurship, and activism. As a first-generation, low-income student studying at Columbia University, I am using my platform to advocate for marginalized communities.

In my free time, I like reading Medium articles, watching cat videos, playing the clarinet, and chilling with my friends.
